OUR STORY

Built by Family. Built for You.

14 private acres in Livingston, TX founded on the belief that every gathering deserves to be extraordinary.

THE PURPOSE OF INSPIRA RANCH

The Name Inspira Is No Coincidence

It has a deep and strategic purpose. The word Inspira is the core of what we seek to achieve: igniting a spark in people, in entrepreneurs, in leaders.

It is a call to action from internal motivation. That spirit of yes you can moves you to leave your comfort zone and dare to do more.

Founded by Melecio Ortega on 14 private acres in the heart of East Texas, the ranch blends natural beauty with full event infrastructure unlike anything else in the region. What started as a vision became a 5 star rated destination for weddings, quinceañeras, corporate retreats, and transformational conferences.
