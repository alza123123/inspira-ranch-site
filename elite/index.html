OUR STORY

The Purpose of Inspira Ranch

The name 'Inspira Ranch' is no coincidence. It has a deep and strategic purpose. The word 'Inspira' is the core of what we seek to achieve: igniting a spark in people, in entrepreneurs, in leaders.

It's a call to action from internal motivation — that spirit of 'Yes, you can!' that moves you to leave your comfort zone and dare to do more. Founded by Melecio Ortega on 14 private acres in Livingston, TX.
